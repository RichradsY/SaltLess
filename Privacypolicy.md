# Privacy Policy (SaltLess)


Effective date: 2026-03-05  
Last updated: 2026-03-05

## 1. Scope

This policy explains how SaltLess handles data when you use the app.

## 2. Data We May Process

When you use SaltLess, the following information may be involved:

- Food and salt-related records you enter in the app
- Subscription and purchase status (handled by Apple platform services)
- Reminder preferences and reminder time settings

## 3. How We Use Data

We use relevant data only to:

- Provide logging, summary, and display features
- Provide reminder features
- Verify subscription status and grant related access
- Improve product quality and stability

## 4. Storage and Sharing

- Your records are primarily stored locally on your device
- We do not sell your personal information
- Except for platform services required for purchase/subscription, we do not use your records for unrelated purposes

## 5. Trial and Subscription Terms

- A 7-day in-app trial is available on first launch
- This trial is not an App Store subscription intro trial
- Monthly and yearly subscriptions are auto-renewable
- To avoid renewal, turn off auto-renewal in App Store account settings at least 24 hours before the current billing period ends

## 6. Your Rights

You can:

- Delete records in the app
- Disable notifications in system settings
- Manage or cancel subscriptions in App Store account settings

## 7. Children's Privacy

SaltLess is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe there is an issue, please contact us.

We may update this policy due to legal or product changes. The latest update date will be shown on this page.
